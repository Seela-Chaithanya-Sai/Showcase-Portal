Revolutionizing Supply Chain Logistics with AI Cloud Systems

Client: Nexa Logistics Ltd. Industry: Transportation & Supply Chain
Project Duration 8 Months
Technologies Used

Business Challenges

The client was battling fragmented data silos across regional warehouses, causing significant operational lag, delivery tracking discrepancies, and over 18% loss in inventory efficiency due to manual dispatch routing.

The Solution

We engineered a real-time smart dashboard tracking system featuring automatic dispatch routing matrix engines, custom API layers uniting old siloed software, and predictive delivery windows powered by Machine Learning modules.
